2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to personal information collected by Crunchy Plum through:

  • the Crunchy Plum mobile application and website (the "Platform");
  • the merchant dashboard and merchant-facing tools;
  • communications between you and Crunchy Plum, including customer support interactions; and
  • third-party tools and services integrated with the Platform, as described in this Policy.

This Policy applies to both Users (consumers) and Merchants. Where a provision applies specifically to one group, it will be labeled accordingly.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide to Us

(a) Account Registration Information
When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, date of birth (for age verification), and password. Merchants additionally provide business name, business address, tax identification number, and other information required for KYC compliance and Stripe Connect onboarding.

(b) Transaction Information
When you make a purchase, we collect transaction details including the deal purchased, purchase amount, and transaction date. Payment card data is processed directly by Stripe and is not stored by Crunchy Plum. We receive limited payment confirmation data from Stripe (such as the last four digits of your card and card type) for transaction records.

(c) Communications
When you contact our customer support team, submit a refund request, or communicate with us through the Platform, we collect the content of those communications and your contact information.

(d) Merchant-Submitted Content
Merchants provide deal descriptions, pricing information, business images, and other content submitted through the merchant dashboard.

3.2 Information We Collect Automatically

(a) Device and Usage Information
When you access the Platform, we automatically collect information about your device and how you interact with our services, including:

  • Device type, operating system, and browser type
  • IP address
  • Pages viewed, features used, and time spent on the Platform
  • Referring URLs and exit pages
  • Crash reports and performance data

(b) Location Data
With your permission, we collect your precise geographic location (GPS data) through the mobile application to show you deals near your current location and improve deal recommendations. You may disable location access at any time through your device settings without losing access to the Platform.

We do not share your precise GPS location data with Merchants or any third party, except as required by law.

(c) Cookies and Tracking Technologies
We use cookies, pixel tags, and similar technologies to operate the Platform and collect usage data. See Section 7 for more details.

3.3 Information from Third Parties

(a) Stripe — We receive payment confirmation and fraud signal data from Stripe in connection with transaction processing.

(b) Google Analytics — We use Google Analytics to collect aggregated data about how users interact with the Platform. For more information, visit policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

9. Your Privacy Rights

9.1 Rights Under Texas Law (TDPSA)

If you are a Texas resident, you have the right to: access your personal information; correct inaccurate data; request deletion; obtain a portable copy; opt out of targeted advertising (we do not engage in this); and non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

9.2 Rights Under California Law (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including the right to know, delete, correct, opt out of sale or sharing, and limit use of sensitive personal information. To submit a California privacy request, contact us at support@crunchyplum.com.

9.3 How to Submit a Privacy Request

Email: support@crunchyplum.com
Subject line: "Privacy Rights Request"

We will respond within 45 days as required by the TDPSA, with a possible 45-day extension with prior notice.

9.4 Appeals

If we decline your request, you may appeal by emailing support@crunchyplum.com with the subject line "Privacy Rights Appeal."

10. Children's Privacy

The Platform is intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at support@crunchyplum.com immediately.
