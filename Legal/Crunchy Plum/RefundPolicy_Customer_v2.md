## Overview

At Crunchy Plum, we want you to feel confident every time you purchase a deal on our Platform. This Refund Policy explains when and how you may request a refund, how refunds are processed, and what happens to your Store Credit if your account is closed or becomes inactive.

**Key principles:**

- Unused deals within their validity period are generally eligible for a refund.
- Expired deals are automatically refunded to your original payment method — you will never permanently lose the amount you actually paid.
- Service fees are non-refundable when refunding to your original payment method.
- Store Credit from your own payments is protected and can always be reclaimed through our Custodial Balance process.
- Redeemed deals are reviewed on a case-by-case basis and are not automatically eligible for a refund.

This Policy is incorporated into and subject to our Terms of Service. Capitalized terms not defined here have the meanings given in the Terms of Service.

## 1. Definitions

**Paid Value** means the amount you actually paid out of pocket for a deal, excluding any Promotional Value.

**Promotional Value** means any bonus or additional value added to a deal by the Merchant beyond your Paid Value (for example, a $50 deal sold for $30 — the Paid Value is $30 and the Promotional Value is $20).

**Service Fee** means the processing fee charged by Crunchy Plum in connection with a transaction, as disclosed at checkout. Service fees are earned upon purchase.

**Store Credit** means a balance held in your Crunchy Plum account that can be applied toward future purchases on the Platform. Store Credit has no expiration date during the life of an active account.

**User-Funded Store Credit** means Store Credit that originated from a refund of your own Paid Value.

**Platform-Granted Store Credit** means Store Credit issued by Crunchy Plum as a promotion, compensation, or goodwill gesture, not originating from your own payment.

**Custodial Balance** means User-Funded Store Credit held by Crunchy Plum on your behalf following account closure or extended account dormancy, as further described in Section 6.

**Redeemed Deal** means a deal for which the full redemption code has been presented to and accepted by the Merchant.

## 2. Refund Eligibility for Unused Deals

### 2.1 General Eligibility
Unused deals — meaning deals that have not been redeemed and have not yet expired — are generally eligible for a refund. Crunchy Plum reserves the right to deny refund requests that appear to violate our Terms of Service, including Section 7 (Personal Use; Prohibition on Resale and Bulk Purchasing).

### 2.2 Validity Period
All deals on the Platform have a stated validity period, not to exceed ninety (90) days from the date of purchase. Refund eligibility under this Section 2 applies while the deal remains within its validity period. Once a deal expires, the process described in Section 5 (Expired Deals) applies automatically.

### 2.3 How to Request a Refund
To request a refund for an unused deal within its validity period, log in to your Crunchy Plum account and submit a refund request through the refund request screen, or contact us at [SUPPORT EMAIL]. We will process eligible refund requests promptly upon verification.

## 3. Refund Methods for Unused Deals

When your refund request is approved, you may choose one of the following two methods:

### 3.1 Store Credit
Your refund will be credited to your Crunchy Plum Store Credit balance.

- **Amount refunded:** Your full Paid Value plus the Service Fee paid on the original transaction.
- **Processing time:** Instant — Store Credit is available in your account immediately upon approval.
- **Usage:** Store Credit may only be used for future purchases on the Crunchy Plum Platform.

### 3.2 Original Payment Method
Your refund will be returned to the payment method used at the time of purchase.

- **Amount refunded:** Your Paid Value only. Service Fees are non-refundable under this method.
- **Processing time:** 5 to 10 business days, subject to your bank or card issuer's processing schedule. Crunchy Plum is not responsible for delays attributable to Stripe, your bank, or your card network.

### 3.3 Selection is Final
Once you select a refund method and the refund is processed, the selection cannot be changed.

## 4. Redeemed Deals

### 4.1 No Automatic Refund
Deals that have been redeemed in full are not automatically eligible for a refund. Redemption is considered final at the time the Merchant accepts your redemption code.

### 4.2 Case-by-Case Review
If you believe exceptional circumstances justify a refund for a redeemed deal — for example, a Merchant failed to deliver the agreed service after accepting your redemption — you may submit a request to [SUPPORT EMAIL] with a written description of the circumstances. Crunchy Plum will review your request in good faith and respond within a reasonable time.

### 4.3 No Guarantee
Crunchy Plum does not guarantee approval of refund requests for redeemed deals. Approval is at Crunchy Plum's reasonable discretion, based on the specific facts and circumstances of each request.

## 5. Expired Deals

### 5.1 Automatic Refund of Paid Value
When a deal reaches the end of its validity period without being redeemed, Crunchy Plum will generally automatically process a refund of your Paid Value to your original payment method. No action is required on your part under normal circumstances.

- **Amount refunded:** Your Paid Value only. Service Fees are non-refundable.
- **Promotional Value:** Any Promotional Value associated with the deal will expire and will not be refunded.
- **Processing time:** 5 to 10 business days from the expiration date, subject to your bank or card issuer's processing schedule.

### 5.2 Expired or Invalid Payment Method
If the original payment method on file is expired, canceled, or otherwise unable to receive a refund at the time of processing, Crunchy Plum will convert the Paid Value into User-Funded Store Credit and notify you at your registered email address. You may then either use the Store Credit toward a future purchase or follow the Custodial Balance claim process under Section 6.6 to request a cash refund to an alternative same-name bank account.

### 5.3 No Action Required
Because all deals on the Platform have a maximum validity period of ninety (90) days — which falls within the standard processing window of our payment processor — automatic refunds to your original payment method are generally available for all expired deals. You do not need to contact us to initiate this refund.

### 5.4 Questions About Expired Deal Refunds
If you have not received your expired deal refund within 10 business days of the expiration date, please contact us at [SUPPORT EMAIL] with your account information and deal details, and we will investigate promptly.

## 6. Store Credit

### 6.1 No Expiration During Active Account
Store Credit (both User-Funded and Platform-Granted) has no expiration date while your Crunchy Plum account remains active, subject to your account remaining in good standing and in compliance with our Terms of Service. Crunchy Plum reserves the right to forfeit Store Credit balances in accounts that have been suspended or terminated for violations of the Terms of Service.

### 6.2 Account Closure — User-Funded Store Credit
If you close your Crunchy Plum account and have a remaining User-Funded Store Credit balance, the following process applies:

(a) **30-day notice period:** Upon initiating account closure, you will be notified of your remaining User-Funded Store Credit balance and given thirty (30) days to either use the balance toward a purchase or request a cash-out before closure is finalized.

(b) **Transfer to Custodial Balance:** If the 30-day period passes without you using or requesting a cash-out of your User-Funded Store Credit, the remaining balance will be transferred to a Custodial Balance held by Crunchy Plum on your behalf. Account closure will then be finalized.

(c) **Claiming your Custodial Balance:** You may claim your Custodial Balance at any time during the custodial period described in Section 6.4 by following the identity verification process in Section 6.5.

### 6.3 Account Closure — Platform-Granted Store Credit
Platform-Granted Store Credit is forfeited and returned to Crunchy Plum upon account closure. Platform-Granted Store Credit has no cash value and is not transferable to a Custodial Balance.

### 6.4 Account Dormancy
If your account has had no login activity for a continuous period of three (3) years, any remaining User-Funded Store Credit balance will be transferred to a Custodial Balance in accordance with Section 6.2(b) and (c). Platform-Granted Store Credit will be forfeited. Crunchy Plum will send a reminder notice to your registered email address sixty (60) days before initiating this transfer.

### 6.5 Custodial Balance — Duration and Reminders
Crunchy Plum will hold your Custodial Balance for a period of three (3) years from the date it is established. During this period:

(a) You may claim your Custodial Balance at any time by following the identity verification process described in Section 6.6; and

(b) No later than sixty (60) days before the end of the three-year custodial period, Crunchy Plum will send a written reminder to your last known registered email address informing you that your Custodial Balance will be reported to the State of Texas if not claimed before the deadline.

### 6.6 Identity Verification for Custodial Balance Claims
To claim your Custodial Balance, send a written request to [SUPPORT EMAIL] from your registered email address. Your request must include the following information for identity verification:

(a) Your registered email address;

(b) The last four (4) digits of the primary payment card associated with your account at the time of account closure or last activity; and

(c) At least one (1) verifiable detail from your purchase history, such as the name of a deal purchased, the approximate date of purchase, or the name of the associated Merchant.

Crunchy Plum will review your request and, upon successful verification, process your refund within 10 business days.

### 6.7 Custodial Balance Refund Methods
Verified Custodial Balance claims will be refunded by one of the following methods, at your election:

(a) **Original payment method:** Refund to the payment card or account on file at the time of your last account activity, if still valid and accessible; or

(b) **Same-name bank account:** Refund to a bank account held in your legal name, for which you provide routing and account information. Crunchy Plum reserves the right to request supporting documentation to verify account ownership.

Service Fees originally paid are not included in Custodial Balance claims and are non-refundable.

### 6.8 Unclaimed Property Reporting
If your Custodial Balance remains unclaimed at the end of the three-year custodial period, Crunchy Plum will report and remit the unclaimed funds to the Texas State Comptroller's office in accordance with the Texas Unclaimed Property Law (Texas Property Code Chapters 72–75). Crunchy Plum may deduct reasonable administrative costs from the Custodial Balance prior to remittance where permitted by applicable law. Once reported and remitted to the State of Texas, Crunchy Plum's obligations with respect to such funds are fully discharged, and Crunchy Plum shall have no further liability to you in connection with those funds. You may claim your funds directly from the State of Texas through the official unclaimed property program at www.claimittexas.gov.

## 9. Merchant Relationship and Refund Funding

### 9.1 Refunds Funded from Merchant Settlement
Refunds processed by Crunchy Plum are funded from amounts owed to the relevant Merchant as part of the settlement process. Crunchy Plum's role in facilitating refunds is purely administrative. Crunchy Plum does not fund refunds from its own assets, and does not guarantee the availability of funds from any Merchant to satisfy any refund request. In no event shall Crunchy Plum be liable for any refund amount that cannot be recovered from a Merchant's settlement balance.

### 9.2 Merchant Bankruptcy or Closure
In the event a Merchant ceases operations, files for bankruptcy, or becomes otherwise unable to satisfy its obligations before your deal is redeemed, Crunchy Plum will use commercially reasonable efforts to facilitate a refund of your Paid Value in accordance with this Policy. However, Crunchy Plum's ability to process such refunds is contingent upon the availability of funds in the relevant Merchant's settlement account. Crunchy Plum shall not be liable for any shortfall resulting from a Merchant's insolvency or failure to maintain sufficient funds. This obligation is subject to the limitations set forth in Section 6.5 of the Terms of Service.
