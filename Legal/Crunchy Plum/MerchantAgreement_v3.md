## 2. Definitions

**Deal** means a discounted offer or promotional package created by Merchant and listed on the Platform for purchase by users.

**Commission Rate** means the percentage of each Deal's Gross Transaction Value retained by Crunchy Plum as its fee for services rendered, as set forth in the commission schedule agreed upon during Merchant onboarding, which may be updated from time to time in accordance with Section 6.3.

**Gross Transaction Value (GTV)** means the total amount paid by a user for a Deal, including the Paid Value and any Promotional Value, but excluding any taxes collected by Crunchy Plum on behalf of the Merchant.

**Net Settlement Amount** means the GTV of completed Transactions minus (a) the Commission Rate, (b) any refunds, chargebacks, or adjustments processed during the settlement period, and (c) any other amounts owed by Merchant to Crunchy Plum under this Agreement.

**Paid Value** has the meaning given in the Refund Policy.

**Redemption** means the act of a user presenting a valid Deal code to the Merchant and the Merchant accepting such code in exchange for the delivery of the goods or services described in the Deal.

**Transaction** means a completed purchase by a user of a Deal listed by Merchant on the Platform.

## 6. Commission and Settlement

### 6.1 Commission
For each completed Transaction, Crunchy Plum will retain a Commission at the rate of **[COMMISSION RATE]%** of the Gross Transaction Value, as agreed between Crunchy Plum and Merchant during the onboarding process. The applicable Commission Rate for each Merchant is specified in the commission schedule provided to Merchant at the time of registration and incorporated herein by reference.

### 6.2 Settlement Cycle
Crunchy Plum will disburse the Net Settlement Amount to Merchant's connected Stripe account on a T+7 basis, meaning that funds from Transactions completed on a given day will be disbursed seven (7) calendar days after the transaction date, subject to:

(a) the Merchant's Stripe Connect account being active and in good standing;

(b) no pending investigations, holds, or disputes related to the Merchant's account; and

(c) the Net Settlement Amount being a positive balance after all deductions under Section 6.3.

### 6.3 Deductions from Settlement
Crunchy Plum may deduct from any settlement payment:

(a) the applicable Commission on all Transactions in the settlement period;

(b) the full amount of any refunds processed during the settlement period in accordance with Section 8;

(c) the full amount of any chargebacks received during the settlement period in accordance with Section 9;

(d) any Rolling Reserve amounts withheld in accordance with Section 6.6; and

(e) any other amounts owed by Merchant to Crunchy Plum under this Agreement.

Refunds and chargebacks shall have priority over any settlement payments to Merchant. Crunchy Plum will first satisfy all outstanding refund and chargeback obligations before disbursing any Net Settlement Amount to Merchant.

Crunchy Plum may set off any amounts owed by Merchant — or by any other business entity under common ownership or control with Merchant — against any current or future settlement payments without limitation, regardless of whether such amounts are in dispute, without prior notice to Merchant. For the avoidance of doubt, this set-off right applies to all debts owed to Crunchy Plum by Merchant under this Agreement or any other agreement, and is not limited to amounts arising from the specific Transactions in a given settlement period.

If deductions exceed the Net Settlement Amount for a given period, the resulting negative balance will be carried forward and deducted from future settlement payments. If a negative balance persists for more than thirty (30) days, Crunchy Plum may require Merchant to remit the outstanding amount directly.

### 6.4 Commission Rate Changes
Crunchy Plum reserves the right to modify the Commission Rate applicable to a Merchant's account by providing thirty (30) days' prior written notice to the Merchant's registered email address. If Merchant does not agree to the revised Commission Rate, Merchant may terminate this Agreement by providing written notice within the thirty (30) day notice period.

### 6.5 No Interest
Crunchy Plum shall not be obligated to pay interest on any funds held pending settlement, except as required by applicable law.

### 6.6 Rolling Reserve
Crunchy Plum reserves the right, in its reasonable discretion, to withhold a rolling reserve of up to **[RESERVE RATE]%** of Gross Transaction Value (the "Rolling Reserve") for a period of up to ninety (90) days following the applicable Transaction date, to cover potential refunds, chargebacks, and other liabilities arising from Merchant's Deals. The Rolling Reserve will be held by Crunchy Plum and released to Merchant on a rolling basis as the associated liability exposure expires, subject to there being no outstanding disputes, refunds, chargebacks, or other unresolved obligations against Merchant's account at the time of release.

Crunchy Plum will notify Merchant at its registered email address if a Rolling Reserve is applied or adjusted. Merchant acknowledges that the Rolling Reserve does not bear interest and is not a deposit or escrow account. Crunchy Plum may increase the Rolling Reserve percentage, or suspend settlement payouts entirely, at any time based on: (a) Merchant's chargeback ratio or refund volume; (b) Merchant's risk profile; (c) unusual or sudden spikes in Transaction volume; or (d) suspected fraudulent activity, deal manipulation, or platform abuse — with prior written notice to Merchant where practicable.

Upon termination of this Agreement for any reason, Crunchy Plum may continue to hold the Rolling Reserve for a period of up to one hundred eighty (180) days following the date of the last Transaction processed for Merchant, to cover any chargebacks, refunds, or disputes that may arise during that period. Any remaining Rolling Reserve balance after such 180-day period, net of all outstanding obligations, will be released to Merchant's connected Stripe account. Retention of the Rolling Reserve beyond this period will be subject to applicable unclaimed property laws.
