## 3. Billing and Charge Calculation

### 3.1 CPC Billing
For CPC campaigns, Merchant's Ad Credit balance will be debited each time a user clicks on Merchant's advertisement. The amount debited per click is the CPC rate applicable to the campaign at the time the click occurs, as set during campaign configuration. Clicks are recorded and counted by Crunchy Plum's platform systems, and Crunchy Plum's records shall be the authoritative source for click counts absent manifest error.

### 3.2 CPM Billing
For CPM campaigns, Merchant's Ad Credit balance will be debited for every one thousand (1,000) impressions delivered. An impression is counted each time Merchant's advertisement is displayed to a user on the Platform. Impressions are recorded and counted by Crunchy Plum's platform systems, and Crunchy Plum's records shall be the authoritative source for impression counts absent manifest error.
