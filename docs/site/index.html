📱 Customer App

The DealJoy app lets you find and buy local deals and coupons near you.

Signing In to DealJoy

What it does: The Sign In screen lets you log into your DealJoy account so you can browse deals, buy coupons, and manage your purchases.

How to sign in with your email

  1. Open the DealJoy app. You will see the Welcome back screen with the DealJoy logo and the tagline "Best local deals in Dallas."
  2. Tap the Email field (the white box below the word "Email"). Your keyboard will appear.
  3. Type your email address — for example, jane@example.com. Make sure there are no extra spaces before or after your email.
  4. Tap the Password field (just below the Email field). Type your password. You will see dots instead of letters — this is normal and keeps your password hidden.
  5. If you want to stay logged in, look for the "Keep me signed in for 30 days" option below the password field. Leave it checked if you don't want to sign in every time you open the app.
  6. Tap the big pink Sign In button.
  7. If your email and password are correct, the app will take you to the home screen where you can start browsing deals.

How to sign in with Google

  1. On the Sign In screen, scroll down past the Sign In button until you see "or continue with".
  2. Tap Continue with Google.
  3. Choose your Google account or sign in to Google if prompted.
  4. You will be taken to the DealJoy home screen automatically.

Tips

  • You can tap the eye icon on the right side of the password field to show or hide your password. This is helpful if you want to double-check what you typed.
  • If you forgot your password, tap "Forgot password?" (in pink text, below the password field) to reset it. You'll receive an email with instructions.
  • If you don't have an account yet, tap "Sign Up" at the very bottom of the screen where it says "Don't have an account?"
  • Signing in with Google is the fastest option — no password to remember.

Common questions

Q: I typed my email but the Sign In button doesn't do anything. What's wrong?
A: Double-check that your email is spelled correctly and that you entered a password. Both fields must be filled in before Sign In will work.

Q: I see "Invalid credentials" after tapping Sign In. What does that mean?
A: Either your email or password is incorrect. Try retyping both carefully. If you still can't get in, tap "Forgot password?" to reset your password.

Q: What does "Keep me signed in for 30 days" mean?
A: If you turn this on, the app will remember you for 30 days. You won't need to enter your email and password every time you open DealJoy. After 30 days, you'll be asked to sign in again for security.

Q: Is it safe to sign in with Google?
A: Yes. DealJoy uses Google's secure sign-in system. Your Google password is never shared with DealJoy.

Creating Your DealJoy Account

What it does: Sets up your free DealJoy account so you can browse and buy local deals in Dallas.

How to use it

  1. Open the DealJoy app. If you don't have an account yet, you'll see the Sign In screen. Tap Sign Up (or Create Account) to go to the registration page.
  2. You'll see the "Join DealJoy" screen with several fields to fill in:
    • Username — Pick a name that's unique to you (letters and numbers only, no spaces).
    • Full Name — Your real first and last name.
    • Email — The email address you want to use for your account.
    • Password — Choose a strong password. If the field turns red, your password doesn't meet the requirements yet.
    • Confirm Password — Type the same password again to make sure it matches.
  3. Scroll down until you see the checkbox that says "I agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy." Tap the empty square checkbox to check it.
  4. Once all fields are filled in and the checkbox is checked, the Create Account button at the bottom will turn from gray to its active color. Tap it to create your account.
Can't tap "Create Account"? If the button looks grayed out and nothing happens when you tap it, you most likely haven't checked the "I agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy" checkbox. Scroll down, tap the checkbox, and the button will become active.

Tips

  • You can tap the blue Terms of Service and Privacy Policy links to read them before you agree.
  • If a field has a red border, something needs to be fixed — check that your password is long enough and that both password fields match.
  • Tap the eye icon next to the password fields to show or hide what you typed. This helps you make sure there are no typos.
  • Already have an account? Tap "Sign In" at the very bottom of the screen instead.

Common questions

Q: Why is the "Create Account" button gray and won't let me tap it?
A: The button stays gray until you check the "I agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy" checkbox. Find the checkbox below the password fields, tap it, and the button will become active.

Q: What if I already have an account with this email?
A: The app will let you know if the email is already in use. If that happens, tap "Sign In" at the bottom of the screen to log in instead, or use a different email address.

Q: Is my account free?
A: Yes. Creating a DealJoy account is completely free. You only pay when you choose to buy a deal.

Search for Deals and Stores

What it does: Lets you quickly find deals and stores near you by typing a keyword — like a restaurant name, food type, or activity — right from the home screen.

How to use it

  1. Open the DealJoy app. You'll land on the Deals home screen (the house icon at the bottom left).
  2. Tap the search bar at the top of the screen. It has a magnifying glass icon on the left side.
  3. Type what you're looking for — for example, BBQ, spa, or pizza.
  4. Wait about one second. Suggestions will appear automatically below the search bar — you do not need to tap a "Search" button.
  5. Use the Store and Deal tabs just below the search bar to switch between results:
    • Store — shows matching businesses (name, address, and phone number).
    • Deal — shows matching deals you can buy.
  6. Tap any result to see more details. Each result has a small arrow (›) on the right side — tap anywhere on the card to open it.
  7. To clear your search and start over, tap the ✕ icon on the right side of the search bar.

Tips

  • You don't need to type the full name. Even a few letters (like BBQ) will show matching stores and deals.
  • Check that your location is set correctly — look at the top left corner of the screen. It should say your city (for example, Dallas, TX). Tap it to change your area if needed.
  • The Store tab is selected by default (shown in red). If you're looking for a specific deal rather than a business, tap the Deal tab.
  • If no results appear, try a shorter or more general keyword. For example, use burger instead of double cheeseburger.

Common questions

Q: I typed something but nothing showed up. What's wrong?
A: First, make sure your location is set to a city where DealJoy operates (shown in the top left corner). If your location is correct, try a different keyword — the store or deal may be listed under a different name.

Q: What's the difference between the "Store" and "Deal" tabs?
A: Store shows you businesses that match your search — useful when you know where you want to go. Deal shows you specific offers you can buy — useful when you're looking for a bargain on a type of activity or food.

Q: Do I need to tap a search button after typing?
A: No. Just type your keyword and wait a moment. Results appear automatically.

🏪 Merchant App

The DealJoy Merchant app lets business owners create deals, manage their store, and redeem coupons.

Signing In to Your Merchant Account

What it does: The sign-in screen lets you log into the DealJoy Merchant app using your email address and password so you can manage your store, deals, and orders.

How to use it

  1. Open the DealJoy Merchant app on your phone. You will see a screen that says "Welcome back" with the DealJoy store icon at the top.
  2. Tap the Email field and type in the email address you used when you registered your merchant account (for example, merchant@dealjoy.com).
  3. Tap the Password field and type in your password. Your password will appear as dots for security.
  4. If you want to double-check your password, tap the eye icon on the right side of the password field. This will show your password in plain text so you can make sure it's correct. Tap the eye icon again to hide it.
  5. Tap the large orange Sign In button.
  6. If your email and password are correct, you will be taken to your merchant dashboard.

Tips

  • Make sure you are using the Merchant app, not the regular DealJoy customer app. The merchant app shows a store icon on the sign-in screen.
  • Your email is not case-sensitive — John@example.com and john@example.com both work.
  • Use the eye icon to reveal your password before tapping Sign In. This helps avoid failed login attempts caused by typos.
  • If you do not have an account yet, tap the orange Register link below the Sign In button to create one.

Common questions

Q: I typed my email and password but nothing happens when I tap Sign In?
A: Check your internet connection. The app needs an internet connection to verify your login. Also make sure there are no extra spaces before or after your email address.

Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?
A: Look for a password reset option on the sign-in screen. If you don't see one, contact DealJoy support and they will help you reset your password.

Q: It says my email or password is wrong, but I'm sure I typed them correctly?
A: Tap the eye icon next to the password field to see exactly what you typed. Make sure you haven't accidentally turned on Caps Lock or that autocorrect hasn't changed a letter.

Q: Can I use my customer account to sign in here?
A: No. The Merchant app requires a separate merchant account. If you only have a customer account, tap Register to create a new merchant account.

Keep your password private. Never share your merchant login with anyone you do not trust. Anyone with your login can access your store settings, view your sales, and manage your deals.

Create Your Merchant Account

What it does: This is the first step to sign up as a DealJoy merchant. You'll enter your business email and create a password to get started.

How to use it

  1. Open the DealJoy Merchant app. You'll see the Create Account screen with a progress bar showing Step 1 of 6.
  2. Tap the Business Email field and type the email address you use for your business (for example, jane@myrestaurant.com). It must be a valid email format — meaning it needs an @ symbol and a domain like .com or .net.
  3. Tap the Password field and create a password. Your password must be at least 8 characters long.
  4. Tap the Confirm Password field and type the same password again exactly as you did above.
  5. Tap the orange Next button at the bottom of the screen to continue to Step 2.
Tip: Tap the eye icon on the right side of the Password field to show your password as you type. This helps you avoid typos.

Tips

  • Use an email you check regularly — DealJoy will send important account and payment notifications there.
  • Pick a strong password: mix uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and a symbol (for example, Tacos2026!). The minimum is 8 characters, but longer is better.
  • If you already signed up before, tap Sign In below the form instead of creating a new account.

Common questions

Q: I tapped Next but nothing happened. What's wrong?
A: Check for a red error message near one of the fields. The most common reasons are: your email is missing the @ or .com part, your password is shorter than 8 characters, or your two passwords don't match.

Q: Can I use my personal email (like Gmail or Yahoo)?
A: Yes. Any valid email address works. A business email looks more professional, but a personal email is fine to get started.

Q: I see "Already have an account?" — what does that mean?
A: If you (or someone at your business) already created a DealJoy Merchant account with this email, you don't need to sign up again. Tap Sign In to log in instead.

Important: Remember the email and password you use here — you'll need them every time you log in to manage your store and deals.
