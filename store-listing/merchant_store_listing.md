## 完整描述（App Store & Google Play，≤4000字符）

**Crunchy Plum Merchant** is the dedicated management app for businesses listed on the Crunchy Plum local deals platform in the Dallas–Fort Worth area.

**Built for Local Business Owners**
Run your Crunchy Plum store from your phone. Create deals, track orders, scan customer vouchers, and monitor your earnings — all in one place.

**Key Features:**

📋 **Deal Management**
• Create and publish deals with custom images, pricing tiers, and descriptions
• Set availability, usage rules, and validity periods
• Edit or pause deals at any time

📱 **QR Code Scanning**
• Scan customer vouchers directly from the app
• Instant redemption confirmation
• Works offline for quick in-store use

📦 **Order Management**
• View all incoming orders in real time
• Track order status and customer details
• Manage refund requests

💰 **Earnings & Payouts**
• View daily, weekly, and monthly revenue
• Track commission breakdown
• Request withdrawals to your bank account

📊 **Analytics Dashboard**
• Today's stats at a glance: revenue, scans, and orders
• Review customer ratings and feedback
• Understand peak hours and popular deals

⭐ **Reviews**
• Read and respond to customer reviews
• Monitor your overall rating and dimension scores (Service, Environment, Product)

🏪 **Store Profile**
• Upload store photos and manage your gallery
• Set business hours and location
• Keep your store information up to date

**Who Is This App For?**
This app is exclusively for merchants who have registered and been approved on the Crunchy Plum platform. Consumer-facing features are available in the separate Crunchy Plum app.

To get started as a merchant, apply at crunchyplum.com/merchant.
