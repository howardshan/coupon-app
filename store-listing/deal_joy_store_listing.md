## 完整描述（App Store & Google Play，≤4000字符）

**Crunchy Plum** is your go-to app for discovering the best local deals at restaurants, spas, salons, and more in the Dallas–Fort Worth area.

**Why Crunchy Plum?**
We believe in zero-risk shopping. Buy any deal, and if your plans change, get a full instant refund — no expiration pressure, no excuses needed.

**What You Get:**
• 🍽️ **Restaurant Deals** — Exclusive set menus and dining packages at top local spots
• 💆 **Spa & Wellness** — Massages, facials, and wellness sessions at great prices
• ✂️ **Beauty & Salon** — Haircuts, nail care, and styling offers near you
• 🎭 **Activities & More** — Experiences you can't find anywhere else

**Key Features:**
• Browse deals by category or location with Near Me search
• Instant QR code vouchers — no printing needed
• Full refund at any time before redemption
• Save your favorites to a personal collection
• Leave reviews to help the community
• Secure Stripe-powered payments
• Order history and coupon management in one place
• Gift a voucher to a friend directly in the app

**How It Works:**
1. Browse deals near you or in your city
2. Purchase securely with a saved card or Apple Pay
3. Receive your QR voucher instantly
4. Show the QR code at the merchant to redeem
5. Changed your mind? Tap "Refund" anytime for an instant refund

Crunchy Plum is built for the Dallas community, by people who love local businesses. Explore new places, support local, and shop with confidence.

Download now and start discovering!
